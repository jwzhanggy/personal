# RedChamber World

**Website:** [https://redchamber.world](https://redchamber.world/)

## Overview

RedChamber World is an AI-native Spatial Intelligence Platform that transforms real-world environments and AI-generated content into intelligent, interactive, and programmable digital worlds. The platform is developed by Prof. Jiawei Zhang's research group at the [IFM Lab](http://www.ifmlab.org).

## What You Can Do

- **Create 3D Worlds from Photos:** Take photos with your smartphone and AI transforms them into fully explorable 3D environments.
- **Generate Content with AI:** Create 3D objects, 3D worlds, images, videos, and music using built-in AI tools — no professional skills required.
- **Explore in Real-Time:** Walk through 3D worlds in first-person on web browsers, mobile devices, VR headsets, and AR glasses.
- **Social & Multiplayer:** Add friends, explore worlds together, voice chat, and interact in real-time.
- **Gaming:** Play multiplayer minigames inside immersive 3D worlds.
- **Create & Earn:** Creators can monetize their content and earn real money through the platform's creator economy.

## Who Is It For

- **Consumers:** Capture real-world locations in 3D with your phone, explore with friends, and interact with AI-powered guides.
- **Creators:** Use AI-powered tools to create content across multiple formats and earn from your audience.
- **Developers:** Build interactive experiences, AI agents, and world logic within programmable 3D environments.
- **Enterprise:** Transform physical spaces into interactive digital twins with intelligent, agent-powered experiences.

## Supported Languages

English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese (Brazil).
